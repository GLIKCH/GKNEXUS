# What is GLIKCH NEXUZ?

GLIKCH NEXUZ is a **privacy-first AI Engineering Platform** built for developers, cybersecurity professionals, researchers, students, and technology enthusiasts who want complete ownership over their AI workflow.

Instead of relying entirely on cloud-based AI subscriptions, GLIKCH NEXUZ allows you to leverage **locally hosted Large Language Models (LLMs)** through LM Studio while providing an advanced desktop workspace focused on productivity, engineering, and project continuity.

This is **not just another chatbot.**

It is designed to become an all-in-one AI workspace.
